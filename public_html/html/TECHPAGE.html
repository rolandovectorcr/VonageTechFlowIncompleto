Add phone to an extension

*Before proceeding, make sure phone has the Ethernet cable connected to the LAN/SW/Internet port and is getting IP address. Furthermore, get the customer's network devices. Based on Network Compatibility list verify if router is compatible , if not educate customer about the issue; however, as an extra mile you will change settings on the router that could be interfering with the services.*

1) Sign in to your Online Account.

2) 2) Click My Extensions icon on the home page. Alternately, click the Phone System icon at the top of the page, and Extensions in the left navigation pane.

3) Click the extension you want to assign to a phone.

4) Scroll to the bottom of the page, and click Devices to expand the section.

5) Select Register Another Device.

6) Check Active box.

7) Select the correct Provisioning Model and enter the MAC Address.

8) Click Save.

9) Click Save at the top or bottom right side of the page.

10) Scroll to the bottom of the page and click Devices again to expand the section and see the device you just registered.

11) Click the here link. You will see a pop-up that says Reset Device Successful.

12) Proceed to Factory Reset the Phone.

Factory Reset

*If passwords don't work, press and hold the speaker button, unplug and plug back in phone, release speaker button, unplug and plug phone.

All Phones with the Exception of Wireless Model (W52P DECT)

From the WEB User Interface (UI)

1) Press the OK button on the front of the phone to acquire the phone’s IP address.

2) Input the IP address into any web browser and press enter.

3) Enter the username and password.

4) Username: admin

5) Password: admin or your Vonage Business account number.

6) Select Settings from the top menu, then Upgrade from the left navigation pane.

7) Click Reset to Factory Setting.

8) Click OK in the pop-up window to complete the factory reset.

From the phone

1) Press and hold the OK key on the face of the phone for 10 seconds.

2) Press OK again to confirm. The phone will prompt you for a username and password.

3) Press the down arrow key and enter your password.

4) Username: admin

5) Password: admin or your Vonage Business account number.

8) Select OK.
